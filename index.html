Syntax Guide

  • \( \sum_{i=0}^{n} i \) sum(i=0, n) i
  • \( \prod_{i=1}^{n} i \) prod(i=1, n) i
  • \( \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} \) lim(x -> inf) 1/x
  • \( \frac{dy}{dx} \) dy/dx
  • \( \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx \) int(a, b) f(x) dx
  • \( \int f(x) \, dx \) int f(x) dx
  • \( \frac{a}{b} \) (a) / (b)
  • \( \alpha, \beta, \gamma, \theta \) alpha, beta, gamma, theta
  • \( e^x \) exp(x)
  • \( x \leq y, x \geq y, x \neq y \) x <= y, x >= y, x != y
  • \( \infty, -\infty \) inf, minf
